Lucia and Jason are the most important unanswered question in GTA 6.

We've seen them do things. We have not seen them *be* people.

## The Current Problem

Lucia is a cipher with excellent cheekbones and a criminal record. Jason has spoken fewer than ten words across two trailers. Their relationship is the emotional core the game is selling, yet we have almost no texture on why they are together or what they want beyond "the score."

Bonnie and Clyde only works if we understand both halves.

## What Trailer 3 Must Show

1. **A real conversation between them that isn't about the job.** Something personal. A fight. A quiet moment. A reveal about one of their pasts.

2. **Individual agency.** We need to see each of them make a decision the other doesn't agree with. Right now they move as a unit.

3. **One of them has a clear flaw or moral line they won't cross.** This is what makes characters memorable in Rockstar games (see Arthur, Niko, Michael).

4. **A hint at the ending.** Not the actual ending, but the thematic stakes. Are they riding off into the sunset or burning each other down?

## The Map Connection

Several of the story locations already on the preview map only make sense if these two have very different backgrounds or goals. The inland areas feel like Jason's world. The strip and port feel like Lucia's. When Rockstar finally shows us the geography of their relationship, the map will make a lot more sense.
