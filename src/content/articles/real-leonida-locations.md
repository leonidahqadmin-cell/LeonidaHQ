Rockstar has a long history of taking real places and making them better.

## The Obvious Ones

- Vice City = Miami / South Beach (the strip, the neon, the art deco).
- The swamps and everglades are straight from the real Florida.
- The inland "second city" has strong Tampa / Orlando / Jacksonville energy in the trailer footage.

## The Map Evidence

Many of the pins we've placed line up with real Florida landmarks that Rockstar has historically used or referenced:

- Major ports and smuggling history.
- Old drive-in theaters and abandoned roadside attractions.
- High-end shopping and entertainment districts that scream "new money Florida."

The preview map isn't random. It's clearly built from real satellite data and on-the-ground research, just like they did for Los Santos and Liberty City.

## Why It Matters

When the game launches, the "I know this place" moments will be constant for anyone who's spent time in Florida. The map preview is giving us a head start on learning the real Leonida before it becomes the most visited virtual state in gaming history.
