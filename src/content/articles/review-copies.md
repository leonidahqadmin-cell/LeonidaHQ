One of the most common questions heading into launch: **when will GTA 6 reviews actually drop?** Nothing is official yet, but the realistic window is easy to map from how major releases typically work.

## The Normal Pattern

For a tentpole game, publishers send **review code to outlets roughly one to two weeks before release**, with a **review embargo** that lifts a few days ahead of — or right at — launch day. For a **November 19, 2026** release, that points to scored reviews landing in **mid-November 2026**, likely the week of launch.

## The Rockstar Wrinkle

Rockstar is historically **tight with pre-release access.** It has often kept embargoes close to launch and limited early hands-on, which means GTA 6 reviews may arrive later and in a tighter cluster than a typical AAA game. Don't be surprised if the embargo lifts just a day or two before the 19th.

## How to Read It

When reviews do land, weigh aggregate scores against your own priorities, and be cautious of any "review" that appears *before* the official embargo — early "leaked review" claims are a classic scam vector. The real verdicts will arrive in a clear, coordinated wave.
